Crystal Collector Game

How to Play

A random number will be displayed at the start of the game. Your goal is to make your points match the random number by choosing the right crystals.

The four crystals below each hold a number value that will increase your points respectively when you click one.

If your points match the random number, then YOU WIN. If your points equal more than the random number given, then you lose.
